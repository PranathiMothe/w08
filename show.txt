The Taj Mahal which is located on the bank of River Yamuna in Agra is one of the top tourist spots in India. 
Being recognized as one of the Seven Wonders of the World, this marvelous mausoleum draws numerous tourists from 
within the country and across the world. 
Not only for its magnificent beauty but also for its history, the Taj Mahal is well-known worldwide. 
It is a soul filled with love, loss and sorrow. 
This glorious monument is an example how intensely a man loved his wife that to keep her memories evergreen, he built a remarkable building like the Taj Mahal.
